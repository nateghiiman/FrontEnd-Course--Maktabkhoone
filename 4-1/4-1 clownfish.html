Clown Fish

A layer of mucus on the clownfish's skin makes it immune to the fish-eating anemone's sting.

scientific name: Amphiprioninae
average lenght: 11 centimeters
average lifespan: 7 years
habitat: Tropical coral reefs

Bright orange with three distinctive white bars, clown anemonefish are among the most recognizable of all reef-dwellers. They reach about 11 centimeters in lenghth, and are named for the multicolored sea anemone in which they make their homes.
